About me

Ever since I was young, I've had a passion to understand how things works and growing up I started developing video game mods and managing unofficial game servers.

Over time I started studying programming languages at school, on manuals and learning from my more experienced colleagues on my first job.


I started working as a technician, developing .NET applications for PDAs and smartphones, I was also a Java teacher for a short time.

Now I'm a seasoned Software Developer with almost 11 consecutive years' experience in the Information Technology. I'm passionate about making the most efficient software for my clients, I've always worked to the best of my ability and I'm continuing to increase my knowledge in the field of web development.
